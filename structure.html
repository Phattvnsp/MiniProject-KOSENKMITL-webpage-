Mechatronics Engineering

The Mechatronics Engineering program integrates knowledge from mechanics, electronics, and computer science, with a focus on robotics. It aims to produce and develop smart technologies for industrial applications.

Engineering Department is an interdisciplinary course that integrates knowledge from mechanical engineering and electronics engineering. This integration allows students to learn about robotics technology, automation systems, control systems, and manufacturing, all taught by expert instructors. Additionally, the program encourages and promotes students to express creative ideas, develop teamwork skills, and enhance management abilities, guided by the SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals) and the 4Cs (Critical thinking, Communication, Collaboration, and Creativity). These principles help students integrate various areas of knowledge with specialized tools and equipment, aligning with current events, technologies, innovations, and industries. This holistic approach also contributes to the development of society and the nation.

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering program focuses on the design, development, application, and problem-solving of computer systems and operations in all aspects. It also aligns with global trends in areas such as AI, IoT, and Network & Cyber Security.

Computer Engineering program integrates knowledge from computer science and engineering, preparing students to design, develop, and solve problems related to computer systems. It covers key areas like AI, IoT, and Network & Cyber Security, aligning with global trends. The program emphasizes creativity, critical thinking, communication, and teamwork, helping students develop leadership and management skills. By applying specialized tools and techniques, students are equipped to tackle real-world challenges, contributing to technological innovations and societal development in line with SDGs.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering program combines electrical and electronic disciplines, applying and developing expertise in these two fundamental areas of engineering. It also includes learning about the use of renewable and clean energy for advancing global technology.

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department focuses on equipping students with in-depth knowledge and expertise in both electrical and electronics engineering. It aims to develop their abilities in designing and advancing electrical power systems and high-level electrical engineering, which are essential for future smart systems. The program also encourages students to apply their engineering and technological knowledge to create innovations, solve problems effectively in electrical and electronics engineering, and develop strong teamwork skills and professional ethics.
